bAKIRE is a food truck in Pharr, Texas bringing you handcrafted matcha lattes, specialty coffee, and our signature build-your-own mini pancakes — all made fresh to order with quality ingredients.

Whether you're craving a creamy Tres Leches matcha or a plate of warm churro pancakes drizzled in cajeta, we've got something special waiting for you.
